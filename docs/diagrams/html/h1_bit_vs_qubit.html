One bit vs one qubit

The whole idea, in one picture. No physics required.
Normal computer
A bit is 0 or 1. You pick one.
Two bits give four combinations, and the computer checks them one at a time.
Quantum computer
A qubit is a blend of 0 and 1 at once, like a spinning coin before it lands.
Add qubits and the space it can hold explodes.
10 qubits1,024 combinations, together
60 qubitsmore than seconds since the Big Bang
300 qubitsmore than atoms in the universe
